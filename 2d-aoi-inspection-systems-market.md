<p><ul><li>Inline 2D AOI</li><li>Offline 2D AOI</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The 2D Automated Optical Inspection (AOI) market is divided into two main types: inline and offline systems. Inline 2D AOI systems are integrated directly into the manufacturing process, enabling real-time inspection and immediate feedback, which enhances efficiency and minimizes defects. In contrast, offline 2D AOI systems operate separately from the production line, allowing for batch inspections after assembly. This flexibility can be beneficial for stringent quality control but may result in longer detection times for issues in the manufacturing process.</p></p>
