<p><p>The CNC welding machine market is divided into two primary types: plastic welding machines and metal welding machines. Plastic welding machines utilize processes like ultrasonic, laser, and hot plate welding to join thermoplastic materials, ensuring strong bonds and precise fabrication in industries such as automotive and packaging. In contrast, metal welding machines focus on techniques like MIG, TIG, and spot welding to fuse metals, catering to sectors like manufacturing and construction. Both types enhance efficiency and ensure high-quality welds in their respective applications.</p></p>
