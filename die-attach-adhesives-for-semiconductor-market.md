<p><p>Die attach adhesives for the semiconductor market are crucial for securely bonding semiconductor dice to substrates. Insulating type adhesives offer electrical insulation, preventing short circuits and enhancing reliability. Sintering type adhesives utilize heat to create a solid bond through particle fusion, providing superior thermal and electrical conductivity. Heat-curing type adhesives harden when exposed to elevated temperatures, forming strong bonds that are essential for high-performance applications. Each type serves distinct requirements based on thermal, electrical, and mechanical properties in semiconductor packaging.</p></p>
