<p><p>The e-scooter market primarily utilizes three battery types: Nickel Metal Hydride (NiMH), Sealed Lead Acid (SLA), and Lithium-Ion (Li-ion). NiMH batteries offer a moderate energy density and are more environmentally friendly but heavier. SLA batteries are cost-effective and robust, yet heavier and less efficient compared to newer technologies. Lithium-ion batteries, encompassing Lithium Iron Phosphate (LFP) and Lithium Polymer (LiPo), are prevalent due to their high energy density, lightweight, and faster charging capabilities, making them ideal for modern e-scooters.</p></p>
