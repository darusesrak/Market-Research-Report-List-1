<p><p>The electric die grinders market is primarily categorized into angle grinders and straight grinders. Angle grinders are designed with a rotating disc set at a right angle, enabling versatile cuts, grinding, and polishing tasks, particularly in tight spaces. Conversely, straight grinders have a straight shaft that facilitates precision in detailed work such as engraving and finishing. Both tools are essential in various industries, including automotive and manufacturing, offering distinct advantages based on user needs and application requirements.</p></p>
