<p><p>The epoxy bushing market is categorized into three types based on pressure specifications: low-pressure, medium-pressure, and high-pressure bushings. Low-pressure bushings are designed for lighter applications, providing suitable insulation and protection at minimal stress. Medium-pressure bushings cater to moderate operational conditions, offering enhanced durability and performance. High-pressure bushings are engineered for demanding environments, capable of withstanding significant electrical and thermal stresses, ensuring reliable functionality in critical applications. Each type addresses specific industrial needs, influencing material selection and manufacturing processes.</p></p>
