<p><p>The flow chemistry reactors market comprises several types, each designed for specific applications. Continuous Stirred Tank Reactors (CSTR) offer thorough mixing and steady-state operation, ideal for reactions needing uniform conditions. Plug Flow Reactors (PFR) ensure minimal back-mixing, promoting efficiency in fast reactions. Micro Reactor Systems (MRT) utilize small-scale reactors for precise control and rapid heat and mass transfer, enhancing safety and reaction rates. Other systems may include batch reactors and hybrid designs, catering to varied industrial needs.</p></p>
