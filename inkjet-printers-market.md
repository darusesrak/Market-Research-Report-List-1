<p><ul><li>Solid Inkjet</li><li>Liquid inkjet</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The inkjet printer market is primarily divided into two types: solid inkjet and liquid inkjet. Solid inkjet printers utilize solid sticks of ink that are melted and converted into a liquid state for printing, offering vibrant colors and environmentally friendly operation. In contrast, liquid inkjet printers use liquid ink cartridges, providing versatility and high-quality prints, suitable for various media. Both types cater to different needs, with solid ink being preferred for commercial applications and liquid ink dominating the consumer market.</p></p>
