<p><strong>The Lab Fume Cupboards Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Conventional Fume Cupboards</li><li>Auxiliary Air Fume Cupboards</li><li>Re-circulatory Fume Cupboards</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The lab fume cupboards market encompasses several types designed to ensure safety in laboratory environments. Conventional fume cupboards provide a primary barrier against hazardous fumes and vapors, effectively ventilating them outside. Auxiliary air fume cupboards enhance airflow by drawing in additional outside air, improving operator safety and comfort. Re-circulatory fume cupboards filter the air and recirculate it within the laboratory, making them suitable for locations with restrictions on external exhaust. Each type serves distinct needs based on safety and operational requirements.</p></p>
