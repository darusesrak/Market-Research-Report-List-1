<p><p>The Non-Fusible Disconnect Switch market features two primary types: Rotary and Knife-blade. Rotary disconnect switches utilize a rotating handle to open or close the circuit, allowing for easy visual confirmation of the switch's status. They are typically used in applications requiring frequent operation. Knife-blade disconnect switches consist of a metal blade inserted into a stationary contact, providing a robust and reliable means to disconnect the circuit. These switches are favored in high-voltage applications for their durability and straightforward design.</p></p>
