<p><strong>The Process Gas Chromatography Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Gas-solid chromatography (GSC)</li><li>Gas-liquid chromatography (GLC)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The process gas chromatography market includes two primary types: gas-solid chromatography (GSC) and gas-liquid chromatography (GLC). GSC involves the separation of gaseous compounds using a solid stationary phase, which interacts with analytes based on adsorption characteristics. In contrast, GLC utilizes a liquid stationary phase on a solid support, where gases partition between the stationary phase and the mobile gas phase. Both methodologies are essential for analyzing volatile compounds in various industries, including petrochemicals, environmental monitoring, and food safety.</p></p>
