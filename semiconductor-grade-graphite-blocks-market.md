<p><p>The semiconductor grade graphite blocks market consists of three main types: extruded graphite, molded graphite, and isostatic graphite. Extruded graphite is produced through a continuous process, offering uniform density and preferred for applications requiring high strength and thermal conductivity. Molded graphite is formed through compression molding, suitable for intricate shapes and high-performance applications. Isostatic graphite combines high purity and performance, achieved through an innovative isostatic pressing process, making it ideal for critical semiconductor applications requiring exceptional electrical and thermal properties.</p></p>
